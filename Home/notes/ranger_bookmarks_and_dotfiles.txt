====== ranger bookmarks and dotfiles ======
//^{Wednesday 28 October 2020}//
@ranger @dotfiles

to create a bookmark in ranger

navigate to the folder;

then type "m" then pick the next number in the sequence. 
to access that bookmark later, press ~ then choose the number corresponding to the folder you wish to navigate to.
